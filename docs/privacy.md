# Privacy

Web MCP is designed so your data never leaves your computer.

## Everything runs locally

- The browser opens on your own machine. It is not connecting to any remote server.
- The MCP server runs on your computer. It does not phone home.
- No browsing data, URLs, or actions are sent anywhere outside your session.

## No tracking

- We do not collect analytics.
- We do not use telemetry.
- We do not track which websites you visit or what you do with the tool.

## Your profile is isolated

Web MCP uses its own dedicated Chromium profile. This means:

- It does not touch your regular Chrome or Edge profile.
- Your saved passwords, bookmarks, and cookies from your main browser stay separate.
- When Web MCP is done, you can delete its profile without affecting anything else.

## Screenshots stay local

When the AI takes a screenshot of a page during a task, that screenshot stays in memory on your machine. It is not stored permanently or uploaded anywhere.

## Summary

Your data is yours. Web MCP does not collect, send, or store anything without your direct instruction.
